The consultation process of the Joint Parliamentary Committee (JPC) on the bill was the largest ever exercise undertaken by a parliamentary panel in India's democratic history, said Rijiju. The bill aims to simplify and modernize various outdated provisions while keeping the core democratic values intact.

Experts have welcomed the initiative, stating that it will promote a more efficient legal ecosystem and improve governance. Meanwhile, stakeholders from various sectors have been invited to provide feedback on the draft.

This article highlights the significance of collective dialogue and participatory policy-making. The success of this bill could lay a strong foundation for more inclusive legislative practices in the future.
